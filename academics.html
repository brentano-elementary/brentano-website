Overview

At the heart of Brentano’s academics, we believe that every child has the potential to succeed. By providing authentic resources such as community outreach and technology, we strive to help each child construct goals and achieve dreams. Through engaging experiments, project based learning, and higher order thinking, Brentano students transform into life-long learners.

Vision

Brentano Math & Science Academy students will become authentic learners and develop appreciation for their own future and success.

Structure

Brentano offers exceptional programs for students in PreKindergarten through 8th grade. Instruction emphasizes math and science curriculum while incorporating literacy instruction into all content areas.

Enroll for Pre-K now!!

Our enthusiastic Pre-Kindergarten Program is dramatically increasing in size and we want you to be apart of this educational growth! PreK provides a morning session that runs from 8:45 to 11:20 am and an afternoon session that runs from 1:05 to 3:40 pm. Our teachers provide circle time, gross motor activities, small group instruction, center choice time, interactive social play, and story time. Two of Brentano’s four PreK sessions are blended learning environments in which students in the general education classroom work side by side with students in the special education program. All PreK teachers are certified in Early Childhood Education and have support from paraprofessionals throughout the day to provide individualized instruction for each student. Our excellent PreK program aims for student happiness in PreK and beyond. Click here for more information on our outstanding PreK Program and the enrollment process.

Diverse learner services

Brentano educators design and implement educational programs that accommodate for each individual student and their specific learning styles. Brentano provides special education services (for those students that are eligible) in PreK through 8th grade.The special education teams work with teachers in the general education setting to provide the best education possible for each student. These accommodations and support are provided within the general education setting as well as though numerous pull-out programs. Please Contact Ms. Nolan for more information on special education services or information for referral.
