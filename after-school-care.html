After-school care

Brentano offers tuition-based before- and after-school care through a partnership with J at School.

Before-school care: 7:00 to 8:45 am, Monday through Friday.

After-school care: 3:45 to 6:00 pm, Monday through Friday.

J at School offers options for daily, weekly, and drop-in care. To learn more, see their application.
