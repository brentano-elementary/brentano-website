Brentano is a growing school and we welcome new students and families to our community. If you are interested in learning more about joining Brentano, please visit for a tour with Principal Lavin.

To schedule a visit, please contact:

Email: SLavin@cps.edu

Call: (773) 534-4100


Kindergarten through 8th grade enrollment

Brentano is a neighborhood magnetic cluster school. All families living within our attendance boundaries may enroll immediately at any grade level K-8. See the Chicago Public School's Map for more details.

Brentano's attendance boundary

Families living outside our attendance area can enroll by applying to Brentano through CPS’ Options for Knowledge Process. Out-of-cycle enrollment may be possible depending on available space. Please call (773) 534-4100 to learn more.

For questions and more information regarding K-8 enrollment, please contact our main office at (773) 534-4100 or email Principal Lavin at SLavin@cps.edu.

Prekindergarten enrollment

To enroll in Prekindergarten, all families apply through CPS’ Ready-to-Learn PreK process in the spring. Out of cycle enrollment may be possible depending on available space. Please call (773) 534-4100 to learn more.

For assistance and further information, contact the main office at (773) 534-4100 or email Principal Lavin at SLavin@cps.edu.
