Enrollment

Welcome Families!

Brentano is a growing school and we welcome new students and families to our community. If you are interested in learning more about joining Brentano, please visit for a tour with Principal Lavin.

To schedule a visit, please contact Mr. Lavin.

Email: SLavin@cps.edu

Call: (773) 534-4100


Neighborhood and out-of-neighborhood enrollment for Kindergarten through 8th grade

Brentano is a neighborhood magnetic cluster school. All families living within our attendance boundaries may enroll immediately at any grade level K-8.

Brentano's attendance boundary

Families living outside our attendance area can enroll by applying to Brentano through CPS’ Options for Knowledge Process.

For questions and more information regarding K-8 enrollment, please contact our main office at (773) 534-4100 or email Principal Lavin at SLavin@cps.edu.

Prekindergarten enrollment

To enroll in Prekindergarten, all families must apply through CPS’ Ready-to-Learn PreK process.

For assistance and further information, contact the main office at (773) 534-4100 or email Principal Lavin at SLavin@cps.edu.
