Enrollment

Welcome Families!

We excitedly welcome new students and families to Brentano. Attendance area is generally as follows (although students are welcome from outside the neighborhood boundaries).

Neighborhood Enrollment

Brentano is a choice school and we would love to show you around! Tours are available Monday thru Friday by appointment, please call Mr. Einbinder at (773) 534-4100.

Enrollment Forms

  1. Two documents to prove address within boundaries (includes but not limited to electric, water, or gas bills, mortgage statement, drivers licensee, etc.)
  2. Original Birth Certificate
  3. Current medical (including immunizations), dental, and vision records

Upon completion of enrollment forms, guardians will also be required to complete the following:

  1. Home Language Survey
  2. Emergency Form
  3. Lunch Application
  4. Media Consent Form

Medical information

Parents that want to bring treats for celebrations must adhere to the following:

  • Brentano is a peanut and nut free school. No home baked products will be accepted.
  • When bringing treats contact your child's teacher ahead of time to set up a date and time. Check with teacher to find out if there are any other allergies in the classroom (eggs, milk, etc.)

High Alert: If your child has asthma and/or allergies to foods or any other health condition you must notify the school, so that a 504 plan can be completed. In addition, you must have the two forms completed and signed by your doctor. Medication that is not accompanied by a doctor's order cannot be given to students. Please call the school (773/534-4100)for further information and ask to speak to the nurse or the case manager.

The forms are here:

  • Emergency Action Plan
  • Physician's Report on Child with Allergies
  • Asthma information and forms

Application Process for Students Outside of the Brentano Neighborhood Boundaries

Brentano also accepts students who have a desire to attend our school but reside outside the Brentano Boundaries. Please contact the main office for more information.

If you have any additional questions regarding the enrollment process, you may call the Brentano Academy Main Office at
