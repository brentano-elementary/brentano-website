Life at Brentano

We know that education is key for student success, therefore Brentano aims to give our students the best well rounded education as possible. So with that end in mind we want our students to LOVE SCHOOL and desire to be part everything we have to offer. Our before and after school programs provide enrichment and fun activites that promote a well rounded education and love for school. The program is staffed by caring teachers that mentor and support our students. Brentano Math and Science Academy provides enriched student life and gives every student as many opportunities as possible to enhance their learning beyond the classroom.

Our Brentano Family is happy to provide a variety of opportunities for every student that attends our school. These opportunities include after school programs, sports, clubs and activities, tutoring programs before and after school, school wide celebrations, volunteer opportunities, community events, and more. The 2013 basketball team was rewarded and recognized this year after completing an outstanding season. The team ended up winning first place in the end of the year tournament.

Currently, the sports programs include flag football, basketball, soccer, softball, track and field, volleyball, cheerleading, and dance. These athletic programs are led by caring mentors who set high expectations for their athletes and provide support both on and off the court. We enjoy providing students with these opportunities and our students enjoy participating. Come and see a game or have your student join in on the fun! Contact Coach Horn for more information.

Brentano Pledge

I pledge to be responsible for my own actions, words, and deeds.
I will use "manner words" such as please, thank you, and excuse me.
I will listen when others speak and will not interrupt the person who is speaking.
I will give only appropriate, positive comments.
I will always be willing to lend a helping hand.
I will respect the property of others.
I will speak in a voice appropriate to the occasion.
I will always be read and willing to learn.

Clubs

Among many support structures for our students, Brentano also has a multitude of after school programs and activities. These include Chemistry Club, Chess Club, Drama Club, Computer Reading Club, Genius Science Club, Girls on the Run (fitness club), Rocket Club, Stellar Girls Science Program, Photography Club, Newspaper Club, and Joffrey Ballet. We also provide sports programs such as boys and girls soccer, track and field, volleyball, cheerleading, dance, basketball, and flag football.
